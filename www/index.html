Gameplay

We're playing a variation on Hide and Seek called Hide and Sock. There are two teams which take turns. We'll email you and let you know which team you're on and which base to meet at.

Hiding

When you're hiding, you wear a sock (VISIBLY) on your hand -- this distinguishes you from the seekers. When you're found, tell whoever found you your secret code -- but keep hiding! The fewer people find you, the more points you get.

Seeking

When you're seeking, you need a small pad and pen to write down the codes of those you find. When you find someone, don't shout! The more people that find that same person, the less points you each get. Finding a skilled hider is more rewarding than finding an unskilled one.

After the Game

At 16:00, when the game is over, come back to this site and enter the codes you collected. We'll send out rankings and winners within a few days. No prizes, sorry. But you can win honour and glory!

Please

  • Stay outdoors! Don't go into buildings. Don't bother cafe owners. Don't hide under cars.
  • Stay smart! Don't go near the Royal Palace. There are armed guards there and they don't want to help you play Hide and Seek. Don't go on private property.
  • Play nice! Don't cheat. Sharing codes lowers the number of points you get. Plus, it's a children's game.
